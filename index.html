I'm IIceeKing — a Product Designer and Creative Developer who believes that truly great software isn't just functional, it's beautiful, intuitive, and unforgettable.

My journey into design and development started over five years ago right here in Lagos, Nigeria — where I taught myself to code and design out of pure curiosity and a burning desire to build things that actually matter. What began as late nights watching YouTube tutorials and reverse-engineering websites I admired turned into a full-blown career crafting digital experiences for startups, agencies, and international clients.

I specialise at the intersection of design and engineering — a rare space where visual precision meets technical execution. I speak both languages fluently: I can articulate a complete design vision in Figma in the morning, and ship the responsive, pixel-perfect front-end by evening. This dual fluency means clients get faster iteration cycles, fewer handoff gaps, and products that actually look and feel the way they were designed to.

My work has spanned e-commerce platforms, fintech dashboards, SaaS applications, mobile apps, brand identity systems, and interactive prototypes. Whether I'm designing a checkout flow that converts, engineering a design system from scratch, or crafting micro-interactions that delight users — I bring the same obsessive attention to detail and the same relentless commitment to quality.

Outside client work, I'm constantly exploring the cutting edge: generative UI, motion design, AI-integrated product experiences, and what the next generation of digital interfaces will look and feel like. I believe the best designers today are also builders — and I'm proudly both.

When I'm not at my desk, I'm mentoring upcoming designers in the Lagos tech community, dissecting Formula 1 strategy, or finding new music. I bring that same energy — precision, speed, and style — to everything I create.
